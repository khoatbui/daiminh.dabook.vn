HANOI – HUONG PAGODA 1 DAY

Perfume Pagoda, the biggest pagoda complex in Vietnam, is about 75 km south from Ha Noi. This hosts the Perfume Festival, a Buddhist event that lasts from the first to the third month by the lunar calendar. All year round, you can enjoy marvelous scenery along the Yen River as well as the historical grandeur of the pagoda complex. On the way, if you fancy an invigorating work-out, you can row the boat by yourself.

PROGRAM

7.30 AM - 8.30 AM: Pick you up at your hotel and depart to Perfume Pagoda. Then you take row-boat trip to go to visit that land on the Yen stream "romantic time" about 1 hour to Thien Tru harbor. Then you will enjoy time to trekking go up and down to visit the main cave which is so nice. Climing step by step on stone stairs, you will arrive at Huong Tich cave on the top of the mountain. You can visit the pagoda inside of the limestone cave, and see amazing formation of stalactites and stalagmites. The walk back to the boat will take about an hour mainly down hills.

Have lunch at the bottom mountain in restaurant beside.

After lunchtime, you will go to visit the Heaven Kitchen pagoda and to learn about Buddhism ancient architectural at Le Dynasty. Then you will come back by row-boat trip and 2 hour road trip to Hanoi and arrive at about 18h30.

Tour duration: 1 full day.

Pick up point: Hotel around the Old Quarter or HANOI OPERA HOUSE (No 1 Trang Tien street)
