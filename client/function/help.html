It is one of the common mistakes to input wrong name, surname, gender, and etc when booking tickets online; due to typing mistakes, or due to mis-checking information, or due to asking other people to do the reservation or booking.

For some minor typing mistakes, the airline may assist passengers to correct it without charge. However, in some cases, passengers will have to pay to correct the information. Airlines may not allow you on board and you may be denied at the immigration if you have incorrect information on your ticket. Thus you need to report to DABOOK.VN or Airline to find a solution right after spotting incorrect information.

If you have lost your foreign, not Vietnamese, passport, here are some documents you can consider as a substitute of your passport.

※ Note: Alternate documents must contain your portrait photo

Below: Diplomatic letter from diplomatic institution, police report of passport loss and clarification letter that your new passport is in re-issuing process, identification issued by competent foreign diplomatic institution, work permit (when boarding Vietnam domestic flight)

If you are a Vietnamese citizen 14 years old and older, you can fly Vietnam domestic routes with a variety of documents with name and valid photos as below:

  1. ID card
  2. Driving license
  3. Proof of the armed forces(Police/Army)
  4. Party membership card
  5. Journalist card
  6. National Assembly deputies card
  7. Aviation security control card
  8. Vietnamese Airlines identification cards
  9. Passport
